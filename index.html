with Linguistics Losses to Reduce Foreign Accents

Speech Samples for the Intelligibility Study

(1) VCC 2020: We select top 4 systems from all submissions. VCC 2020 only converts English speech [1].
(2) In-House System: Our in-house XVC system, which converts both English and Mandarin Speech [2].

Source: The natural speech from a source speaker, we hope to preserve the speech content and native accent.

Intra-Lingual:
Both source and target speakers speak the same language.
Cross-Lingual:
The source and target speakers speak different languages.
